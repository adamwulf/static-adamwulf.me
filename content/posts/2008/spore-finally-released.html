Ok ok, I’m a few hours early, but come on – awesome! I don’t know about you, but I’m just a tiny bit excited about this!

Versions for game consoles such as the Nintendo Wii are in the early stages of research and development, though the company isn’t ready to say when those might become available. A version that could be played in a browser window is also under consideration.
